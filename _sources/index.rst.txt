How to Use
==========

.. raw:: html

   <div class="toc">
   <ul>
   
   <li><strong>Learn by doing:</strong> Try solving each problem first. Only check solutions after genuine effort.</li>
   <li><strong>Understand patterns:</strong> Don't memorize‚Äîunderstand why each grammar works. This lets you solve new problems.</li>
   <li><strong>Study PDA after CFG:</strong> Compare PDA solutions with equivalent CFG solutions. They recognize what grammars generate.</li>
   <li><strong>Trace derivations:</strong> Manually trace through each example. Draw derivation trees on paper.</li>
   <li><strong>Study impossibilities:</strong> Questions 26-32 prove what PDAs/CFGs cannot do. This knowledge is as important as knowing what they can do.</li>
   </ul>
   </div>
